DSBAMUN V

DSBA is hosting its fifth bilingual MUN conference from 6th-8th of November 2025. Every MUN conference that DSBA delegates have attended has seen them leave their imprint. Having said that, we are eager for delegates from all schools to visit and leave their mark at ours. Our Secretariats have selected topics that will thrill every committee due to how passionate and heated the debates will be. Our academic committee was carefully and wisely selected to ensure that the brightest and most knowledgeable chairs would be in charge of each committee.

We are looking forward to having you join us!
